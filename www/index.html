How do I find my CSRF?

Open a terminal and type:

$ curl "https://abcdefghij.spotilocal.com:4371/simplecsrf/token.json" -H "Origin: https://embed.spotify.com"

Then copy your token value:

{ "token": "02basd30s9b09s834nbs09384b0d98r9" }

And paste it into the CSRF textbox below!
